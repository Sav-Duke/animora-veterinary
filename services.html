Wellness & Preventative Care

We provide physical exams, vaccinations, parasite control, and tailored nutrition plans to keep pets healthy. Regular visits help us detect conditions early and improve quality of life.

Dental Care

Our dental services include scaling, polishing, extractions, and guidance on maintaining oral hygiene. Good dental care prevents infections and ensures overall wellbeing.

Surgery & Emergency Care

We handle routine surgeries as well as emergency cases. Our clinic has modern facilities and provides anesthesia monitoring, pain management, and compassionate recovery support.

Field Treatment

We provide door-to-door veterinary services for livestock, ensuring prompt treatment for injuries, illnesses, and health emergencies at your farm.

Artificial Insemination

Our advanced reproductive techniques help improve genetics and reproduction rates in livestock herds, ensuring higher productivity and healthier offspring.

Breed Selection

We offer expert advice to help farmers choose the right breeds that match their environment, market goals, and productivity needs.

Vaccination Programs

Protecting herds from diseases through scheduled vaccination programs designed to reduce outbreaks and ensure long-term herd health.

Lab Tests

Our diagnostic lab provides blood, urine, and fecal tests to ensure accurate diagnosis and targeted treatments for pets and livestock.

Disease Screening

We identify and control potential diseases early through comprehensive screening, preventing them from spreading among pets or livestock.

Reproductive Health

Our reproductive services include fertility testing, pregnancy diagnosis, and monitoring, helping animals reproduce safely and efficiently.

Nutrition Guidance

We create personalized feeding plans to meet the specific needs of pets and livestock, ensuring balanced nutrition for growth, health, and productivity.

Farm Management

We provide hands-on training and advisory services in sustainable farming practices, animal husbandry, and efficient resource management.

Disease Prevention

Our preventive strategies include vaccination, deworming, hygiene programs, and monitoring to reduce risks of disease outbreaks in pets and herds.
